About

StartupFair.LA, previously named Bruin Startup Fair, aims to connect UCLA’s best and brightest students to premier rising startups in the Los Angeles area. It’s a great way for entrepreneurial-minded students to interact with companies, offering an excellent opportunity for students to find full-time positions and internships in a close-knit startup environment. We hope to see you there!

This will serve as an incredibly exciting opportunity for you to spread the word about your company. This is one of the few startup-exclusive events at UCLA, so take advantage of this opportunity!

For more info, contact us at startupfairla@gmail.com.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is StartupFair.LA?

StartupFair.LA is an awesome way for students to discover and interact with small companies to find full-time or internship positions. Likewise, startups can speak directly to talented UCLA students at a unique recruitment event.

Who can attend?

The event is open to any student currently at UCLA with a Bruin Card. If your company is interested in sponsoring, sign up to our form here for more details.

What should I bring?

All you need is your Bruin Card, multiple copies of your resume, and business casual clothing to attend.

What if I have more questions?

We're here to help! If you have any questions, feel free to email us at startupfairla@gmail.com or reach out to us on Facebook. Questions about sponsorship? Please contact us at startupfairla@gmail.com.
